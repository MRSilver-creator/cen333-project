About the Application

What is SafeRoute?

SafeRoute is a community-driven safety platform that combines crowdsourced hazard reporting with Gemini AI intelligence and Google Maps to help citizens navigate their city safely — day or night.

GPS-Accurate Reporting

Submit hazard reports pinned to your exact GPS location with a single tap. Coordinates are captured automatically.

Gemini AI Classification

Every report is instantly analysed by Gemini AI — classifying the hazard and routing it to the right authority.

Live Safety Heatmap

See all community reports overlaid on a live Google Maps heatmap. High-severity zones glow red.

Safe Route Planner

Enter your destination — SafeRoute checks for active hazards along the way and offers AI safety advisories.

Community Verification

Upvote accurate reports and dispute false ones. Reports with 3+ upvotes are marked verified.

Contribution Leaderboard

Earn points for every report, verification, and upvote. Rise through the community leaderboard.
